Q: What are your delivery charges?

A: Delivery is free for orders above $50. A $5 delivery fee applies for orders below $50.

A: Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days, while express delivery takes 1-2 business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

A: Yes, we ship to selected countries with additional shipping charges based on the destination.
